# HeadBlocks v3.1.0

## What's New

### ✨ New Features

- **Zone behavior**: confine players to a cuboid or WorldGuard region while playing a hunt. New `headblocks.zone.bypass` permission and GUI config.
- **Timed time limit**: countdown to finish a timed run, teleport back to start on timeout, with optional progress reset.
- **Apply rewards to whole hunt**: copy a head's rewards to every head of the hunt.
- **Hidden leaderboard players**: exclude specific players from `/hb top`.
- New placeholder `%headblocks_hunt_<huntId>_finishers%` to display the number of players who completed a hunt.

### 🚀 Improvements

- Timed start plates are protected from breaking and removed when the hunt is deleted.
- Head placement/assignment refuses or skips heads outside a hunt's zone.
